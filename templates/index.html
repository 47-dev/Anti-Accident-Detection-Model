What are we catering?

To counter present day challenges of accidents, specially in hilly areas, we aim to build a mechanism which inculcates Deep Learning Algorithms that autonomously detect and alert on any possibility of accidents. This is one of the kind model which imbibes structures of process that work remotely, without any hardware mechanism and with the highest accuracy offered.
